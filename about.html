my yoga style

Amanda first began studying yoga 15 years ago at a Dublin yoga studio. She was fortunate to find a great teacher and began a daily practice that lasted several year. In 2011, Amanda went travelling to India and Nepal to deepen her practice, where she studied at the Rishikesh school of yoga, completing a teacher training course over three months.

Amanda now teaches four types of yoga classes: Beginners, Intermediate, Advanced and Hatha style. Classes are held in a small, cosy studio near the city centre and there is a class available for all levels. Why not try a drop-in class and experience the benefits of this lovely practice.s
